Why GBP is Mission-Critical for Titan Roofing

Roofing is a "near me" category — when someone needs a roofer, they search "roofer near me," "roofing companies near me," or "roof repair near me." Google's answer to these queries is almost always the local 3-pack (Google Maps) — not the organic blue links. Titan Roofing currently shows impressions for all three of these terms (884 + 653 + 1,011 = 2,548 combined impressions) but only 6 total clicks. That means the GBP listing is either not appearing in the map pack, or is being outranked by competitors.

Review Strategy

Google Reviews are the #1 local ranking factor. More reviews + higher ratings = higher map pack position = more "near me" clicks. Every 10 new reviews can push a listing up 1–2 spots in the local 3-pack.

Getting to 100 Reviews — Action Plan

  • SMS follow-up: Text every customer within 24 hours of job completion: "Hi [Name], thanks for choosing Titan Roofing! If you're happy with your new roof, a quick Google review helps us a lot: [link]"
  • Email sequence: Day 1 thank you + review request. Day 7 follow-up if no review.
  • QR code: Print Google review QR code on invoices, receipts, and business cards.
  • Respond to all reviews: Reply to every review (positive and negative) within 24 hours. Google rewards responsive businesses.
  • Flag fake/competitor reviews: Report any suspicious reviews to Google immediately.
  • Never incentivize reviews: Against Google's terms of service. Just make asking part of the process.
